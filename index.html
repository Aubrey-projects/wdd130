My Mission in Ecuador

Depending on where you are in Ecuador, it is like a completely different country. There is the coast, which was my first location, where the weather is hot and humid most of the time. Where I served, it was also where most of the poorer people lived and crime was more prevalent. I had many close calls with wild dogs, but I survived just fine. Then there are the mountains. This is the more touristy area where people would come to visit. I served here the latter half of my mission. The weather is cooler and it was much weathier with less crime.
